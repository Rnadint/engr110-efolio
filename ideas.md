## Selected Design: Academic Editorial (Response 2)

**Rationale:** An ENGR 110 eFolio is fundamentally an academic document. The editorial approach conveys scholarly credibility while remaining visually engaging. The warm ivory + deep navy palette feels professional without being cold. Playfair Display headings give the site a distinct, non-generic identity that avoids "AI slop" aesthetics.
